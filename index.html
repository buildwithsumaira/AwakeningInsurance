FAQs

Common Questions,
Clear Answers

What types of vehicles are covered?
+
We cover a broad range of vehicles including personal cars, SUVs, trucks, motorcycles, commercial vans, box trucks, semi-trucks, and specialty vehicles. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific vehicle type.
Do you offer nationwide coverage across all 50 states?
+
Yes! Awakening Insurance LLC is licensed to serve clients in all 50 states. Whether you're in California, Texas, New York, or anywhere in between — we have coverage options available for you through our network of national carriers.
How fast can I get a quote?
+
Most clients receive a tailored quote within 24 hours of submitting their request. For straightforward coverage needs, we may be able to provide quotes even faster. Simply fill out our quote form or call us directly for the quickest service.
Can I switch providers easily if I already have insurance?
+
Absolutely. Switching insurance providers is straightforward, and we handle much of the process for you. We'll review your current coverage, identify any gaps or savings opportunities, and help you transition to your new policy seamlessly — often with a prorated refund from your prior insurer.
Do you insure commercial fleets?
+
Yes, commercial fleet insurance is one of our specialties. Whether you operate 2 vehicles or 200, we can structure a fleet policy that covers all your vehicles under a single plan, with tailored liability, collision, and cargo coverage options for your specific business needs.
What coverage types are available?
+
We offer liability coverage, collision coverage, comprehensive coverage, uninsured/underinsured motorist protection, medical payments coverage, and more. Our advisors will walk you through every option to build the right policy for your situation and budget.
Is there a cost to get a quote?
+
No, all quotes are completely free and come with no obligation. We believe in transparent, no-pressure service. Request as many quotes as you need and take your time deciding.

Our Story

An Agency Born From
a Belief in Better

Awakening Insurance LLC was founded on a simple but powerful idea: that every American deserves access to the best vehicle insurance options available — not just the options that one company happens to sell.

As an independent insurance agency, we partner with multiple nationally recognized carriers to shop the market on your behalf. That means less work for you and better outcomes — more coverage, better rates, and a policy that actually fits your life.

Based in Elk Grove Village, Illinois, we proudly serve clients across all 50 states. From individual drivers in small towns to large commercial fleets operating nationwide, Awakening Insurance is the name clients trust to protect what matters most.
